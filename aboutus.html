ABOUT US

Welcome to W Travels


Embark on unforgettable journeys with W Travels, your gateway to exploring the wonders of the world with ease and elegance. Since our inception in 2010 in the vibrant city of New York, W Travels has been dedicated to crafting bespoke travel experiences that cater to the dreams and desires of our discerning clientele.


Our Mission


At W Travels, our mission is to bridge the gap between dream destinations and discerning travelers through personalized, sustainable, and exceptional travel planning services. We believe in creating not just vacations, but lifelong memories, by meticulously curating experiences that immerse our clients in the culture, beauty, and uniqueness of each destination.


Our Story

Founded by world traveler and entrepreneur, Alex Johnson, W Travels was born out of a passion for discovery and a deep-seated belief in the transformative power of travel. From our first group tour to the vineyards of Tuscany to today's global network of destination specialists, our journey has been driven by a desire to exceed expectations and redefine what it means to travel well .


Our Team


Our team of travel experts brings together a wealth of knowledge, experience, and a shared love for exploration. From our travel planners to our customer service specialists, each member of the W Travels family is dedicated to ensuring your travel experience is seamless, safe, and utterly unforgettable.
