BANGKOK





Explore the grandeur of Wat Pho, one of Bangkok's oldest and largest temple complexes, famous for its massive reclining Buddha statue, which measures 46 meters (150 feet) in length. Admire the intricate architecture, colorful murals, and beautiful pagodas within the temple grounds.





Shop 'til you drop at the Chatuchak Weekend Market, one of the largest outdoor markets in the world, offering a vast array of goods, including clothing, accessories, handicrafts, antiques, and more. Get lost in the maze of stalls, bargain with vendors, and sample delicious street food along the way.





Experience the vibrant atmosphere of Asiatique The Riverfront, a sprawling open-air shopping and entertainment complex located along the Chao Phraya River. Browse through boutiques selling fashion, accessories, and handicrafts, enjoy live performances, and dine at waterfront restaurants with scenic views.
