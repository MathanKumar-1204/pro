THAILAND





Explore the cultural hub of northern Thailand in Chiang Mai, known for its ancient temples, vibrant markets, and picturesque countryside. Visit Wat Phra That Doi Suthep, an iconic temple perched on a mountain overlooking the city, and explore the historic old town with its moat and ancient walls.





Immerse yourself in the natural beauty of Khao Sok National Park, one of Thailand's oldest and most diverse rainforests. Explore lush jungle trails, go kayaking or bamboo rafting on Cheow Lan Lake, and spot wildlife such as gibbons, elephants, and hornbills.





Dive into the underwater world of Koh Tao, a paradise for scuba diving and snorkeling enthusiasts. Discover vibrant coral reefs teeming with marine life, including tropical fish, rays, and even whale sharks, and enjoy the laid-back island atmosphere.
