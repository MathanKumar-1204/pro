INDIA





Visit the Agra Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage site and a historical fort in the city of Agra. Explore its magnificent architecture, including the Jahangir Palace, Diwan-i-Khas, and Moti Masjid. Explore Fatehpur Sikri, a UNESCO World Heritage site located near Agra. Discover its well-preserved Mughal architecture, including the Buland Darwaza, Jama Masjid, and Tomb of Salim Chishti.





Experience the serene beauty of the Kerala Backwaters, a network of interconnected canals, rivers, lakes, and lagoons in the state of Kerala. Enjoy a houseboat cruise through the backwaters, passing by picturesque villages, lush greenery, and coconut groves.





Explore the scenic beauty of Kashmir, often referred to as "Paradise on Earth." Visit the picturesque Dal Lake in Srinagar, take a shikara ride on the lake, and explore the Mughal gardens like Shalimar Bagh and Nishat Bagh. Experience the serene beauty of the Himalayan landscapes, with snow-capped peaks, lush valleys, and meandering rivers.
