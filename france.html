FRANCE





Visit the stunning island commune of Mont Saint-Michel, located off the coast of Normandy. Explore the medieval abbey perched atop a rocky outcrop, wander through narrow streets lined with historic buildings, and admire panoramic views of the surrounding bay.





Experience the beauty of Provence's lavender fields, especially during the summer months (June to August). Drive through scenic countryside roads lined with vibrant purple blooms, visit charming villages like Sault and Valensole, and capture breathtaking views of the rolling hills dotted with lavender.





Step back in time at the Palace of Versailles, a UNESCO World Heritage site and one of France's most iconic landmarks. Explore the opulent palace interiors, including the Hall of Mirrors and the Royal Apartments, and wander through the magnificent gardens adorned with statues, fountains, and manicured lawns.
